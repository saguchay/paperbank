What is Paper Bank?

Paper Bank is a comprehensive document management solution designed to help businesses of all sizes transition from paper-based to digital workflows. Our software and services streamline document storage, retrieval, and collaboration, making it easier for teams to work efficiently and securely.

With Paper Bank, you can centralize all your documents in one secure location, automate routine tasks, and ensure compliance with data retention policies. Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Paper Bank

What types of documents can I manage with Paper Bank?
Paper Bank supports all common document types including PDFs, Word documents, Excel spreadsheets, PowerPoint presentations, images, and more. Our system can handle everything from contracts and invoices to technical drawings and HR documents.
How secure is my data with Paper Bank?
We take security seriously. Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols. We also offer role-based access controls, audit trails, and comply with major data protection regulations.
Can I try Paper Bank before purchasing?
Absolutely! We offer a 14-day free trial of our software so you can experience its benefits firsthand. Contact us to set up your trial account.
How does the document digitalization process work?
Our digitalization process involves four key steps: 1) Document collection and preparation, 2) High-quality scanning, 3) OCR processing to make text searchable, and 4) Indexing and categorization for easy retrieval. We handle everything from pickup to delivery.
What support options are available?
We offer multiple support tiers. Basic plan customers receive email support with a 24-hour response time. Professional and Enterprise plans include priority phone and email support, with Enterprise customers receiving 24/7 assistance critical issues.
